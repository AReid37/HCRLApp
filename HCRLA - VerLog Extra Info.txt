Example 1 Version Log
	PURPOSE
		This verion log example tests for the regular function of the Log Decoder with the correct information entered within version log sections. Very little or no garbage data; looking for ideal, flawless function.
	TESTING OBJECTIVES
	. Correct detection of sections of the version log
	. VERSION specifics
		- A number in the format 'a.bb' exists
	. ADDED specifics
		- Key substitution within lines of added content
		- Identification of content names and their related data ids
		- Content names are checked to be spaced ('ItemZero' becomes 'Item Zero')
	. ADDITIONAL specifics
		- Identification of additional content within their lines, and the added contents they relate to
		- Content names and optional names are checked to be spaced (not to be mistook with non-(alpha)numeric data Ids)
	. TTA specifics
		- A number exists within tag
	. UPDATED
		- Detection of no data (should be similar across ADDED and ADDITIONAL sections)
	. LEGEND
		- Identification of legend key and definitions
	. SUMMARY
		- Identification and partitioning of summary parts
